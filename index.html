Home About Era Med

Built to Serve

Founded in 2019 in Addis Ababa, Era Med emerged from a heartfelt mission: to make reliable, high-quality medical care accessible across Ethiopia.

Grown by Purpose

In 2019, we began with 5 million ETB in revenue and a bold vision. By 2024, we reached 134 million ETB — a reflection not just of numbers, but of lives changed. Clinics stocked, patients served, jobs created — our growth is measured by impact, not income.

Our Vision

We envision an Ethiopia — and a world — where every person, regardless of income or location, has access to reliable, high-quality healthcare. Our vision is to become a leading force in Africa's medical future by championing innovation, local capacity, and ethical impact. At Era Med, we see a future where healing is not a luxury, but a right — and we're committed to building that future, one life at a time.

Our Mission

We believe healthcare is a right, not a privilege...

Click to read more

We believe healthcare is a right, not a privilege. Through innovation in biomedical engineering and pharmaceuticals, we're closing the gap between need and access — one product, one life, one community at a time. Every life matters — and quality healthcare should never be out of reach. Through advanced biomedical engineering and pharmaceutical innovation, we work to close the gap between need and access. Our commitment is not only to deliver trusted medical products, but to restore dignity, hope, and health where it's needed most.
